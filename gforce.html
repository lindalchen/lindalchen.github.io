Project G-Force

Project G-Force is 5 day space-themed Zoom webinar series with WiSTEM meant to introduce girls to the world of engineering and empower them with skills relevant to the field and beyond! This project was created as a community service project from the Zed Factor Fellowship to address the gender disparity within the aerospace and techology industry. All content presented in the webinar will be open-source and posted on this page for anyone seeking resources to pursue a career in STEM.
